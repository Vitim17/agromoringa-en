Meet Agromoringa

Pioneering and excellence in
moringa cultivation

Agromoringa, based in Parnaíba, Piauí, Brazil, is the leading supplier of certified Moringa oleifera seeds for Brazilian agribusiness. We are the only company with a seed cultivar registered with MAPA, ensuring superior genetics and proven results.

Founded by Arthur Henrique Begliomini (Agronomic Engineer, soil and moringa specialist, international reference) and Rhayana Aline (Commercial/Operations CEO, rural producer, psychologist and Vice President of the Brazilian Moringa Association), we combine science, practice and passion for farming.

Why moringa?

Proven benefits for
forage and animal nutrition

Moringa oleifera is the future of the protein bank in Brazilian agribusiness. Discover the scientific and economic advantages.

High productivity

Up to 200 tons of green biomass per hectare per year under a cut-and-carry system. Year-round production.

Rich in protein

25–30% crude protein in leaves. Essential amino acids, vitamins and bioavailable minerals.

Drought tolerant

Proven performance in arid and semi-arid regions. Ideal for diversifying production.

Better performance

Higher milk yield, weight gain and feed conversion. Lower supplement costs.

Sustainability

Nitrogen fixation, recovery of degraded pastures and carbon capture.

Profitability

Fast ROI with multiple products: forage, seeds, meal, pellets and silage.
